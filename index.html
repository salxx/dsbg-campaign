Encounter: Fort Inner Gate

Encounter Introduction

Your party reaches the giant gate that leads to the innermost part of the fort.
A group of Hollows lingers at its wake. Something seems odd about them...

Encounter Setup

Prepare the 'BlaBla' encounter card.
Replace a Crossbow Hollow with a Firebomb Hollow.
You may enter the tile from any side.

Encounter Rules

Defeat all foes.
Count the number of rounds. A round starts on enemy activations.
